§04 — How It Works

Brief to launch in 21 days.

A clear three-stage process so you always know exactly where your project is. The 21-day timeline isn't a waiting period — it's the quality-control window that keeps your site from launching broken.

01

Discovery Call

A focused conversation about your business, your customers, and what your site actually needs to do. I turn that into a written plan with a fixed price and a launch date. Nothing gets built until you've approved it.

02

Design Preview in 72 Hours

I send you a working preview within three days — so you can click around your site before I write a single line of production code. Any changes happen here, before they get expensive. No surprises at the end.

03

Build, Test, Launch

I build your site, then spend a full week stress-testing it — every page, every device, every load time. Security checked. Performance benchmarked. Forms tested end-to-end. Your site goes live bulletproof on day 21 — not patched together and hoped for the best.
